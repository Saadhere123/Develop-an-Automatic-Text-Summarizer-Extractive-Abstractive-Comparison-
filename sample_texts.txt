Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries by automating tasks and improving efficiency. Many companies are adopting AI to improve productivity and decision-making. AI is used in healthcare, finance, education, and many other sectors.

Machine learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data. It is widely used in recommendation systems like Netflix and Amazon.

Natural Language Processing enables machines to understand human language. It is used in chatbots, translation, and sentiment analysis.

Deep learning uses neural networks with many layers. It is powerful in image recognition and speech processing.

Data science combines statistics and computing. It helps businesses make data-driven decisions.

Cloud computing allows storage and processing over the internet. It reduces infrastructure cost.

Cybersecurity protects systems from attacks. It is essential in the digital world.

Big data refers to large datasets. It helps in predicting trends and behaviors.

Blockchain is used for secure transactions. It is popular in cryptocurrency.

Internet of Things connects devices. It enables smart homes and cities.
